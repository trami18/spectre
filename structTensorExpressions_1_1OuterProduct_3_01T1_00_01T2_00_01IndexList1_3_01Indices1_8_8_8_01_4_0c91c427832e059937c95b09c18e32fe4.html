Helper struct for computing the multi-index of a component of an operand of the outer product from the multi-index of a component of the outer product.

Details

While OperandTensorIndexList will either be the first operand's generic indices, ArgsList1<Args1...>, or the second operand's generic indices, ArgsList2<Args2...>, and both of these are accessible within the class, this struct wraps the core functionality and is templated with OperandTensorIndexList so that the functionality can be reused for each operand's generic index list.
